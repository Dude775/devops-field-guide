

Hands-On 6.4 conclusion

NodePort Service was created for the Color API.
The Service received an internal ClusterIP and also exposed nodePort 30007.

On Minikube Windows, direct NodeIP:NodePort is not used.
Access was tested through:
minikube service color-api-nodeport --url

The app was reachable from outside the cluster through a localhost tunnel.
Multiple API requests returned different hostnames, proving load balancing between Pods.

NodePort is useful for development/testing, but not the preferred production exposure method.
