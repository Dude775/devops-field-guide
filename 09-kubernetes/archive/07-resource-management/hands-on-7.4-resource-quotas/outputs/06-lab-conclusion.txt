

Hands-On 7.4 conclusion

Created Namespaces:
- dev
- prod

Created ResourceQuotas:
- dev-quota in dev
- prod-quota in prod

dev quota:
- requests.cpu: 1
- requests.memory: 1Gi
- limits.cpu: 2
- limits.memory: 2Gi

prod quota:
- requests.cpu: 2
- requests.memory: 2Gi
- limits.cpu: 4
- limits.memory: 4Gi

Observed behavior:
kubectl get resourcequota -A shows quotas in both namespaces.
kubectl describe resourcequota shows Used=0 because no workloads are consuming quota yet.

Important point:
ResourceQuota defines namespace-level limits. Actual enforcement becomes visible when Pods with requests and limits are created.
