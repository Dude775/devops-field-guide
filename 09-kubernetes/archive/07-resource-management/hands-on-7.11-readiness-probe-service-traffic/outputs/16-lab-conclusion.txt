

Hands-On 7.11 conclusion

Created:
- color-api Deployment with 6 replicas
- color-api-svc Service
- traffic-generator Pod

Image:
- idf775/color-api:1.2.1

Probe configuration:
- Startup Probe: /up
- Readiness Probe: /ready

Failure simulation:
- FAIL_READINESS=true
- readiness result is calculated at startup with about 50% chance to fail

Observed behavior:
- 6 color-api Pods were created
- only 2 Pods became Ready
- 4 Pods stayed Running but Not Ready
- Restart Count stayed 0 for all Pods

Service behavior:
- Service selector was isolated with lab=readiness-probe to avoid matching older color-api Pods
- Service endpoints included only Ready Pods:
  - 10.244.0.231:80
  - 10.244.0.233:80

Traffic proof:
- traffic-generator logs showed traffic only to the Ready Pod hostnames:
  - color-api-5db48dc649-v4kzm
  - color-api-5db48dc649-snzbv

Important point:
Readiness Probe failure does not restart containers.
It only removes Not Ready Pods from Service traffic.

Cleanup:
- resources from this lab were deleted with kubectl delete -f .

Conclusion:
Readiness is a traffic-management mechanism, not a restart mechanism.
