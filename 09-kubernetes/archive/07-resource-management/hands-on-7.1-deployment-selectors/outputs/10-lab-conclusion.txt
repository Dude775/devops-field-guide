

Hands-On 7.1 conclusion

Created Deployment:
- color-api-deployment
- replicas: 3
- image: lironefitoussi/color-api:1.1.0

Selector used:
matchLabels:
- app=color-api
- environment=local
- tier=backend

matchExpressions:
- key: managed
- operator: Exists

Observed behavior:
The Deployment created 3 Pods.
The created Pods include the labels required by the selector:
app=color-api, environment=local, tier=backend, managed=deployment.

Important point:
matchLabels checks exact key/value labels.
matchExpressions can add more advanced conditions.
Here, operator Exists means the label "managed" must exist.

Conclusion:
The Deployment selector and the Pod template labels match correctly, so the Deployment can manage its Pods safely.
