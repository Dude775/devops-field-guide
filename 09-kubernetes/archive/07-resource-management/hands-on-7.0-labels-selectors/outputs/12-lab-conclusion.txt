

Hands-On 7.0 conclusion

Created two Pods:
- color-backend with app=color-api, environment=local, tier=backend
- color-frontend with app=color-api, environment=local, tier=frontend

Tested:
- label columns with kubectl get pods -L
- equality selector: app=color-api
- equality selector: tier=frontend
- AND selector using one -l flag: app=color-api,tier=frontend
- set-based selector: tier in (frontend)
- set-based selector: tier notin (frontend)

Important observation:
Using multiple -l flags did not behave like AND in this environment.
The reliable AND syntax is:
kubectl get pods -l 'app=color-api,tier=frontend'
