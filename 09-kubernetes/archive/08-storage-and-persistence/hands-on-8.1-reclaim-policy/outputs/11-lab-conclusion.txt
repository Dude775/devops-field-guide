

Lab: Kubernetes StorageClass ReclaimPolicy

Goal:
Keep a dynamically provisioned PersistentVolume after deleting its PVC,
without changing the existing StorageClass.

What happened:
- StorageClass standard uses ReclaimPolicy: Delete.
- A PVC named reclaim-demo-pvc was created with storageClassName: standard.
- Kubernetes dynamically created a PV.
- The new PV initially had persistentVolumeReclaimPolicy: Delete.
- The PV reclaim policy was patched to Retain after creation.
- The PVC was deleted.
- The PV was not deleted and moved to Released state.

Key point:
Do not change an existing StorageClass in production.
Patch the specific dynamically-created PV after it exists.

Result:
PASS - PV was retained after PVC deletion.
